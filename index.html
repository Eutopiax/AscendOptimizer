Abstract

In this paper, we introduce a dual-stage optimization framework driven by Large Language Models (LLMs) to automatically discover and optimize compute operators for the Ascend NPU architecture. Inspired by search-based programming, our agent interacts with the CANN compiler compiler feedback to iteratively refine Ascend C code. We demonstrate significant performance improvements across a comprehensive benchmark of 133 distinct operators.
